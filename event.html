ENTRY REQUIREMENTS

Upon entry, all attendees are required to either: Provide proof that they are fully vaccinated against COVID-19. “Full vaccination” means 14 days or more following the final dose of a US FDA approved or WHO recognized vaccine against COVID-19 (e.g., Moderna, Pfizer, Johnson & Johnson, AstraZeneca/SK Bioscience, Serum Institute of India/COVISHIELD and Vaxzevria, Sinopharm, and Sinovac).
OR
Provide proof of a negative COVID-19 test. The test must be taken no earlier than 72 hours (3 days) prior to your first entry into the festival. (e.g for entering the festival on Sat, November 6th, the test must be taken on Wed, November 3rd or later. If entering the festival for the first time on Sun, November 7th, the test must be taken on Thurs, November 4th or later) If entering the festival on Saturday, negative result will be honored for both festival days.
FACE MASK REQUIREMENT
In accordance with the August 16, 2021 County of Los Angeles Department of Public Health Order of the Health Officer, attendees must wear face masks at all times, except when actively eating and drinking.
