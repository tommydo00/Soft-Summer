ABOUT

Get ready to vibe out at the biggest music festival
of the year! Soft Summer is all about bringing you shows
you'll remember forever and moments that you just
can't miss. We have a star-studded lineup for everyone to enjoy.


We are located at the beautiful Rose Bowl venue in
Pasandena, CA. Whether you are coming here by plane,
train, boat, car, or bus- it's easy to get to Soft Summer.
Located in Brookside, we are only a 30-minute drive
from LAX. We look forward to seeing you here!
